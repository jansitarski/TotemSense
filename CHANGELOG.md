### Added
- Initial release of CCPlates
- Tremor Totem icon display on nameplates for fear/charm/sleep casting NPCs
- Coverage for TBC raids: Karazhan, Gruul's Lair, Magtheridon's Lair, SSC, TK: The Eye, Hyjal Summit, Black Temple, Zul'Aman
- Coverage for TBC dungeons: Hellfire Citadel, Coilfang Reservoir, Auchindoun, TK dungeons, Caverns of Time, Magisters' Terrace
- Coverage for Classic raids: MC, Onyxia, BWL, ZG, AQ40, Naxxramas
- Coverage for Classic dungeons: Scholomance, Stratholme, UBRS, LBRS, BRD
- Configurable icon size, position, and texture
- O(1) NPC lookups with hash-based database
